Mouthwatering Homemade Meatballs

Description

These quick and easy meatballs are the perfect addition to your favorite spaghetti, for making a delicious meatball sub, or as a delicious protein-filled snack!

Yields: 12 meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Ground Beef
  • 1 1/2 cups breadcrumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 white onion diced
  • 4-6 cloves garlic diced
  • 1 tsp. Black pepper
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1 tsp. oregano
  • fresh basil ribbon sliced
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 2 tbsp. milk
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. In a large pan, heat oil over medium heat.
  2. In a large bowl combine your ingredients.
  3. Mix either by hand or with a wooden spoon until all ingredients are well combined. The texture should feel slightly wet.
  4. Form meat mixture into approximately 2" balls and add to hot pan.
  5. Sear all sides of meatballs in pan. Reduce heat to medium low and cook until an internal temperature of 135 degrees fahrenheit.
  6. Enjoy in your favorite pasta, sandwich, or all by themselves!
